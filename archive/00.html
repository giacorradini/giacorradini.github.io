Hitting the Reset Button

Gianmarco Corradini, 16.07.2026

If you noticed that my previous articles are gone, you are right. I decided to delete them and hit the reset button.

For a long time, I wrote about finance and economics. However, over time, I grew deeply unsatisfied with traditional economic models. They are often incredibly abstract, built on theoretical assumptions that look great on paper but collapse in the real world.

When I stopped just reading theories and actually began testing them empirically, the results were eye-opening: the models performed terribly. Writing about unverified theories without rigorous empirical validation no longer felt honest or useful to me.

Moving forward, this space will change. I want to focus on what is tangible, testable, and real—combining my finance background with practical app development and data that actually works.

Thank you for being part of the journey. Stay tuned for what’s next.
