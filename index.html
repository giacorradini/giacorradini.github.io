About Me

Hi, I'm Gianmarco, I work in Frankfurt’s banking sector as a Financial Accountant, with professional experience in financial reporting, tax accounting, and international accounting under HGB and IFRS. Beyond my work, I am passionate about finance, statistics, and programming, and I enjoy building projects that combine analytical thinking with technology.

This website is where I document my journey, share the projects I build, and explore the ideas that interest me. It is organized into two sections:

Archive is a collection of my technical work, including software projects, articles, scripts, and other resources related to finance, accounting, data analysis, and programming.

Journal is a more personal space where I write about my travels, experiences, reflections on life, philosophy, and the lessons I learn along the way.
